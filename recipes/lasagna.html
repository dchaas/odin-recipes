Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a family favorite your kids will be sure to love! This mouth-watering dish consists of 3 layers of pasta, cheese, meat and tomato sauce.

Best when baked to golden brown and served warm.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 32 oz Tomato Sauce
  • 2 cups Mozzerella Cheese
  • 2 lb of ground beef
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 cup of paremsean
  • 1/2 tsp of salt

Steps

  1. Brown beef in a saucepan
  2. Cook lasagna noodles in a separate pan according to the directions on its package.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 F and grease a 9x13 baking dish.
  4. Pour 1/2 cup of sauce in bottom of baking dish.
  5. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/2 cup of meat and 1 cup cheese.
  6. Repeat layers and top with parmesean cheese.
  7. Cover and bake for 35 minutes or until cheese is golden brown.
