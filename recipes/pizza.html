Pizza

Description

Pizza is a family favorite your kids will be sure to love! This mouth-watering dish consists of dough, cheese, sauce and toppings.

Best when baked to golden brown and served warm.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Flour
  • 1 cup of Water
  • 1/2 tsp Yeast
  • 2 cups Mozzerella Cheese
  • 32 oz tomatoe sauce
  • 1 package of pepperoni
  • 1 green pepper

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water and yeast. Let dough rise.
  2. Spread out dough on pizza pan.
  3. Spread tomatoe sauce over dough.
  4. Shred mozzerella and sprinkle over sauce.
  5. Evenly space pepperonis on top of cheese.
  6. Bake at 425 F for 15 minutes or until cheese is golden brown.
