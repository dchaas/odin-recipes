Tacos

Description

Tacos are a family favorite your kids will be sure to love! This mouth-watering dish consists of tortillas, seasoned beef and toppings.

Best when served with salsa and sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 12 flour tortillas
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • shredded lettuce
  • 3 tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Brown beef in a saucepan.
  2. Stir in the seasoning mix, cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Assemble tacos by placing beef in tortilla and topping with lettuce, tomato, sour cream and salsa.
  4. Serve with guacamole.
